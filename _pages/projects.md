### 🌟 Mechanics of Stretchy Elastomer Lattices

**Keywords:** computational mechanics · elastomer lattices · mechanical metamaterials · finite deformation · hyperelasticity · FEM validation

- Applied the affine deformation assumption within a continuum mechanics framework to connect macroscopic lattice deformation with local beam-level kinematics, and decomposed lattice deformation into axial stretching and bending modes.
- Incorporated neo-Hookean, Ogden, and Arruda--Boyce hyperelastic constitutive models to construct the total free energy of elastomer lattice structures from stretching and bending contributions.

- Generated representative lattice geometries in SolidWorks and performed ABAQUS finite element simulations to validate nonlinear stress--stretch predictions across material models and lattice topologies.

- Formulated hierarchical lattice mechanics as a constrained optimization problem in configurational Riemann space, subjected to geometric compatibility constraints, and uniaxial stress-free lateral constraints to explain hierarchy-enhanced macroscopic stretchability.
